About the Realm

The Wizard Realm is a short adventure styled after the early internet and 90s point and click games wherein a wizard merchant gives you quests.

After the Wizard's Introduction, you may select his various trinkets and search the site for hidden words to whisper to the Orb by typing them into the text box.

Upon giving the code to the orb, you progress the quest! There are a total of [INSERT NUMBER HERE] Magic Words.
